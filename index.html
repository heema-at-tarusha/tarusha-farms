Scientific Growing

Why Hydroponics?

Revolutionizing urban food systems with intelligent design, soil-free technology, and optimal resource usage.

Up to 90% Less Water

Our recirculating systems utilize water efficiently, preventing evaporation and reducing water consumption by up to 90% compared to traditional farming.

Faster Growth Cycles

Direct delivery of balanced nutrients to the root zone enables crops to grow up to 3x faster, yielding earlier harvests and more cycles per year.

No Soil Required

Eliminate soil-borne diseases, weeds, and heavy tilling. Grow cleaner plants on water and inert growing media like coco-peat or clay balls.

Grow Year-Round

Control the growing environment and nutrient supply to harvest fresh greens throughout the year, completely insulated from seasonal challenges.

Fresh Harvest at Home

Pick herbs and leafy greens from your kitchen, balcony, or living room seconds before cooking. Experience ultimate freshness and rich flavor.

Reduced Food Miles

Growing hyper-locally prevents transportation carbon footprint, storage degradation, and nutrient loss. Zero transport means zero waste.

Sustainable Urban Farming

Empower cities to feed themselves. Turn empty rooftops, balconies, and unused building spaces into productive urban community green farms.

Better Space Utilization

With vertical systems and compact profiles, you can harvest up to 10x more produce per square foot than traditional horizontal agriculture.

Flagship Innovation

STEM Hydroponic Kit

Our flagship starter kit is engineered to introduce beginners, families, and offices to clean indoor farming without complex pumps, wiring, or loud noises. Beautifully designed to blend with premium interiors.

  • Beginner friendly: No prior agricultural experience required. Setup assistance available in NCR, Vitual guidance availabe PAN India.
  • Water Saving: Uses up to 90% less water than conventional soil farming.
  • Grow Anything: Ideal for fresh herbs (basil, mint, oregano) and leafy salad greens.
  • Low maintenance: Only needs nutrient refilling once every 2-3 weeks.

About Tarusha

TarUshais a founder-led urban farming organization passionate about making clean, organic food cultivation accessible inside dense urban spaces. Based in Gurgaon, India, we blend agricultural technology with beautiful aesthetic designs to empower families, corporate hubs, and campuses.

We focus deeply on sustainability (saving up to 90% water compared to soil farming), education (empowering students with STEM botanic courses), and community impact by localizing crops and mitigating food transit carbon footprints.
